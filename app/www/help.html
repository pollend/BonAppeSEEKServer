Let's get started

Begin this application by clicking “START”
*(IMAGE: START with finger; NEXT)
The first page that appears will provide three drop-down menus
* The top menu provides options of what characteristic your child prefers, with the options of TEXTURE, TASTE, or COLOR
* The second menu will then have a list of subcategories, depending on which characteristic is chosen from the first menu
* The third menu contains the options for parents to specify which food group he or she wants. If there is no preference for food group, the “NO PREFERENCE” will be selected
After a preference is chosen for each drop-down menu, select FIND YOUR FOOD!
*(IMAGE: Drop down menus with finger; NEXT)
A list of food will then be presented matching the criteria previously selected. Select a food and click NEXT
*(IMAGE: List of foods with finger)
The nutrition facts for the selected food will then be presented.
*(IMAGE: Nutrition facts)
PEC BOARD:
To access the PEC board, click the board icon located at the top left of the screen.
*(IMAGE: any page with PEC board icon and finger pointing)
ADDING ITEMS TO PEC BOARD:
The first way of adding food to the PEC board is on the screen with list of foods. Click the “+” on the right side of the foods to add to the PEC board.
*(IMAGE: list of foods and plus signs with finger)
Another way to add items to the PEC board is on the page of each food’s nutritional facts. To add to the PEC board, click the “+” located at the top of the screen.
*(IMAGE: food/nutritional page with the + sign and finger)
You will be notified if your PEC board is full when more than four items have been added.
PEC BOARD:
To delete items from your PEC board, click the “x” located on the top right of the food
*(IMAGE: PEC board and food with x and finger)
To return back to the home page, click the house icon located at the top left of the screen
*(IMAGE: sample screen, finger pointing to home button)
To go back a page, click the arrow icon located at the top left of the screen
*(IMAGE: sample screen, finger pointing to arrow)
